OpenHealth is a beautiful, open-source health dashboard that reads your data from Android Health Connect — and keeps it completely private.

COMPREHENSIVE HEALTH DATA
• Steps, distance, floors climbed
• Heart rate, resting heart rate, HRV
• Sleep sessions with stage breakdown (Awake, Light, Deep, REM)
• Blood oxygen (SpO2), respiratory rate
• Blood pressure, blood glucose, body temperature
• Body composition (weight, body fat, BMR, bone mass, water)
• Calories burned, nutrition & macros
• Exercise sessions with type detection

SMART INSIGHTS
• Daily Readiness Score based on HRV, sleep, and activity
• Stress & Energy tracking
• Sleep Bank (7-day debt/surplus tracking)
• Sleep Clock visualization
• Step streaks and trends

AI HEALTH ANALYSIS (Optional, Bring Your Own Key)
• Get personalized health insights powered by Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, or any OpenAI-compatible API
• Your key, your data — we never see either

HYDRATION TRACKING
• Log water intake throughout the day
• Daily goal tracking with ring gauge

8 BEAUTIFUL THEMES
• 4 dark + 4 light themes

100% PRIVATE
• All data stays on your device
• No accounts, no registration, no cloud sync
• No analytics, no ads, no tracking SDKs
• API keys encrypted with AES-256
• Fully open source (GPL-3.0)

Source code: https://github.com/bune1991/OpenHealth
